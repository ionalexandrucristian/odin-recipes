Mushrooms with butter baked in the stove!

Description

The mushrooms with butter recipe is a great recipe for when in hurry and wanting to eat something good, healthy, and most importantly, fast!

The recipe is really easy to make!

Ingredients

  • 1kg Champignon mushrooms
  • 3-4 garlic 'heads'
  • Enough butter to fill the insides of the mushrooms - melted
  • Salt and pepper - depending of the taste

Steps

  1. Wash the mushrooms very well and peel them
  2. Remove the insides of the mushrooms (can be used for other recipes)
  3. Mince the garlic
  4. Melt the butter
  5. Fill the insides of the mushrooms with butter, garlic and salt and pepper
  6. Add the mushrooms in the stove for about ~20 minutes
  7. Most importantly, ENJOY! You deserve it!
