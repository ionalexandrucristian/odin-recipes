French fries with fried eggs!

Description

The french fries combined with fried eggs is something that can be done in about ~20-30 minutes!

The recipe is really easy to make and very very tasty, although not very healthy! But hey - we only have one life!

Ingredients

  • 3-4 big potatoes
  • 2 eggs
  • Some oil - of course
  • Salt and pepper - depending of the taste

Steps

  1. Wash the potatoes very well and peel them
  2. Try to dry the potatoes, so when they'll get in contact with the boiling oil, it will not spill everywhere!
  3. When the oil is boiling, with care add the potatoes and fry them until golden!
  4. Fry the eggs - 'eyes' style, as we call them here in Romania.
  5. Place the eggs over the french fries!
  6. Most importantly, ENJOY! You deserve it!
